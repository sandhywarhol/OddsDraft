# OddsDraft — Pitch Script (per slide, ~2.5 min)

Matches the 7-slide Canva deck. Target run time **2:30–3:00**. Say the bold line with conviction; everything else can flex.

---

## Slide 1 — Opening  *(~20s)*

> "Hi, we're **OddsDraft**. A billion people watch the World Cup — and they watch it *passively*.
> We turn that into a game: **fantasy football that pays out in real, on-chain SOL prizes**, powered by the live TxLINE feed.
> Three verbs — **Bet, Play, Own**. Pick five players, join for 0.1 SOL, and your score moves the moment the real match does."

---

## Slide 2 — The Problem  *(~25s)*

> "Web3 sports today has three gaps.
> **One — prediction is a commodity.** It's all 'guess the score.' No depth, nothing that brings a user back after the whistle.
> **Two — there's no game, so only die-hards show up.** Pure prediction rewards football knowledge, not play — casual users have nothing to *do*, and they bounce.
> **Three — the value dies at full-time.** You get a payout and nothing to keep. No asset to own, upgrade, or trade.
> **Fans want to bet, play, and collect around one live match — in one place they can trust.**"

---

## Slide 3 — The Solution  *(~30s)*

> "That's OddsDraft — betting, a game, and a card economy on one TxLINE live feed.
> **Play:** build a five-a-side squad, pick a captain for 2×, set confidence stars, and **equip Skill Cards to each player** to boost their points.
> **Bet:** three contest modes — 50/50, Top 3, and Winner-Takes-All — and you can enter all three on the same match.
> **Own:** earn Skill Cards across eight rarity tiers, combine and upgrade them, and trade them for SOL on our marketplace.
> **Betting, game, and economy — all settled on-chain, all driven by the live match.**"

---

## Slide 4 — How It Works (1 of 2)  *(~20s)*

> "Here's the engine. Step one — the **TxLINE live feed**: confirmed goals, cards, saves and stats are our single source of truth.
> Step two — the **scoring engine**: points come from individual performance, match events, and half-time and full-time stats. Goals are position-weighted — a keeper scoring pays 20, a striker 10 — then captain doubles it, confidence multiplies it, and your equipped cards add on top."

---

## Slide 5 — How It Works (2 of 2)  *(~20s)*

> "Step three — the **live leaderboard** re-ranks every squad in real time, with NPC commentary and Telegram alerts firing within seconds.
> Step four — **on-chain payout**: the prize pool is distributed on Solana, and we cryptographically verify that our scores match the TxLINE oracle stream. **Transparent, trustless — no black box.**"

---

## Slide 6 — Monetization  *(~25s)*

> "Three revenue streams off one engaged audience.
> **One — a small fee on every prize pool**, across all three modes; it scales with entries.
> **Two — card sales:** a cut on every marketplace trade, with real scarcity — the top card drops at 0.3%.
> **Three — in-app ads:** a persistent sponsor slot below the nav bar, brand-safe inventory on a live-sports crowd.
> And the timing is perfect: **64 World Cup matches, three modes each — a full month of built-in engagement.**"

---

## Slide 7 — Close  *(~15s)*

> "Our whole thesis in one line: **turn a match you're already watching into a game you're playing.**
> It's live on Solana devnet at **oddsdraft.fun** — you can try it on a live match right now. Thank you!"
